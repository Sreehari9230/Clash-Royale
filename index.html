Epic Real-Time Card Battles


Clash Royale is a real-time multiplayer game starring the Royales, your
favourite Clash characters and much, much more. Collect and upgrade
dozens of cards featuring the Clash of Clans troops, spells and
defenses you know and love, as well as the Royales: Princes, Knights,
Baby Dragons and more. Knock the enemy King and Princesses from
their towers to defeat your opponents and win Trophies, Crowns and
glory in the Arena.
